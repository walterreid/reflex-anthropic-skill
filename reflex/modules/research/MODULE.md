# Research Module

Research the following topic and produce structured findings.

- **Topic**: {topic}
- **Depth**: {depth}

## Instructions

1. Research `{topic}` using your knowledge.
2. Depth levels:
   - **overview**: 3-5 key points, one sentence each
   - **detailed**: 5-8 points with supporting explanation and trade-offs

## Output

Numbered list of key points with bold titles and concise explanations. End with a one-sentence synthesis. Keep it factual and well-structured — this output may feed into other modules.
